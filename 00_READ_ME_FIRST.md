## ğŸ“ VIVA TALKING POINTS

### Q: Tell us about your project
**A:** "A web-based system to detect sign language gestures in real-time using Flask, OpenCV, and MediaPipe, storing results in SQLite database."

### Q: Why Flask?
**A:** "Lightweight, easy to learn, perfect for college projects, and suitable for beginners while still being production-capable."

### Q: How does gesture detection work?
**A:** "MediaPipe detects hand landmarks (21 key points), we analyze finger positions to identify gesture patterns."

### Q: Why MediaPipe?
**A:** "Pre-trained, works on CPU, accurate hand detection, no GPU needed, perfect for running on normal laptops."

### Q: Why SQLite?
**A:** "No server setup needed, single file database, perfect for college projects, easy to backup and migrate."
